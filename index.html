🃏 The Mystery Card

Each player gets 1 mystery card (blue, sideways). At any time, instead of following suit, play your mystery card. It plays as its actual rank & suit.

Once used, normal euchre rules apply for the rest of the hand.

♠ Standard Euchre Rules

Trump: Right bower (J of trump) > Left bower (J of same color) > A > K > Q > 10 > 9

Scoring: Makers need 3+ tricks for 1 pt, sweep 5 = 2 pts. Euchred = opponents get 2 pts.

Stuck: Dealer must name trump in round 2.
